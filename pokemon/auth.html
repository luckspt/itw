Before playing this game you must be aware of the following rules:

You start the game in a small village on pokémon world. Your objective is to go through the village, in the least time possible, until you reach a final destination.
Throughout the map live a myriad of hidden pokémon species ready to jump you if your paths cross. You and your chosen pokémon must fight bravely and catch them with your Poké Ball.
